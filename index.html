Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we have a team of elite certified personal trainers who offer 1-on-1 coaching, tailored workout blueprints, and nutritional guidance. You can book a free assessment session to see if it's right for you.

What are the gym's peak hours?

Our busiest hours are typically between 6:00 AM - 8:30 AM and 5:30 PM - 8:30 PM. Since we are open 24/7, coming outside these windows guarantees a much quieter, private workout experience.

Is parking available at the facility?

Yes, we provide free dedicated parking for all our members right outside the facility, monitored by 24/7 security cameras so your vehicle is always safe while you train.

Do you have locker rooms and showers?

Absolutely. We offer premium locker rooms equipped with clean, private showers, fresh towels, and grooming amenities for your convenience before or after work.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining.

Do I need prior gym experience?

Not at all! Over 40% of our new members have never consistently worked out before. We offer complimentary orientation sessions to teach you how to use every piece of equipment safely.

Are lock-in contract terms required?

No. We operate purely on a month-to-month basis. We want you to stay because you love the results, not because a piece of paper forces you to. You can cancel anytime with a 7-day notice.

Can I freeze my membership if I travel?

Yes! You can pause your membership for up to 3 months per year at zero cost. Just let the front desk know via WhatsApp or email before you leave.
